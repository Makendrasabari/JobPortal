About Us

Who We Are

Stackly is a premium, automated talent matchmaking ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between technical software specialists, systems builders, and organizations who build outstanding products.

Reimagining Tech Recruitment

Founded in 2024, Stackly was born out of frustration with spammy recruiting agencies and opaque job boards that waste time. We believed there was a better way to connect qualified developers with companies who value solid architecture and clean coding.

We built a centralized matching pipeline that evaluates candidates on actual competencies, past contributions, and career expectations. Today, thousands of software engineers, DevOps experts, and product leads use Stackly to build relationships with growing startups and enterprise companies.

Our Mission

To eliminate hiring friction by matching software developers and technical managers with relevant, verified opportunities in real time. We focus on transparency, providing clear salary baselines, technology specifications, and responsive interview steps for every single role.

Our Vision

To build the world's most trusted developer employment ecosystem. We look forward to a future where engineering placement doesn't depend on buzzword-filled resume filtering, but rather on verified portfolio capabilities, mutual interests, and team culture matches.
